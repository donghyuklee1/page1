Undergraduate Student, KAIST

I am sophomore student in the School of Electrical Engineering at KAIST (double major in Physics), advised by Iksung Kang.

My research interest includes generative models for Science, with a strong interest in Computational Optics and the design of optical systems. My current focus is on high-resolution imaging through computational correction of spatially variable aberrations for extremely wide FOVs.

Outside of research, I'm interested in audio equipment and regularly attend listening sessions for new gear and codecs. I also drum, listen to jazz, and absolutely love skiing.
